About Us

Welcome to the "Catdog" Animal Shelter, a non-profit organization committed to providing a loving and safe environment for animals in need. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome abandoned pets, giving them a second chance at life.

Our shelter has been operating for over 10 years, with the help of dedicated volunteers and generous donors. We believe every animal deserves a loving family, and we work tirelessly to match our animals with the right adopters.

At "Catdog," we pride ourselves on being a community-driven shelter that encourages responsible pet ownership and animal welfare. Join us in making a difference in the lives of these beautiful animals!
